About the Firm

Where expertise meets personal attention.

J Dinesh & Co is a Bengaluru-based chartered accountancy firm built on the belief that every client — individual or corporate — deserves the same quality of financial guidance once reserved for large enterprises.

Led by CA Dinesh Jagarlamudi, the firm brings deep expertise in Indian taxation law, GST compliance, corporate structuring, and audit — serving salaried professionals, entrepreneurs, MSMEs, and corporates.

With dual engagement as Founder of J Dinesh & Co and as a practicing CA at Essae Global, Dinesh brings rare corporate finance exposure alongside the responsiveness of a boutique practice.
